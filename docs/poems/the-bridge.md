# The Bridge

*Kjell Silverstein*

--

I said a thing
on this side of the water.
Just one small thing,
not two.

It walked across
on three different bridges
and everyone heard it
come through!

The first bridge asked it questions.
The second bridge carried it home.
The third bridge caught it tripping
and gave it
its actual name.

I never learned
what bridges speak.
I don’t know how they’re made.
I said my thing
on this side once—

and everywhere,
it stayed!
